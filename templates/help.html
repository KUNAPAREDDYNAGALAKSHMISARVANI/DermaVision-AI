How Can We Help You?

Getting Started

1 Upload a clear image of the affected skin area.
2 Crop the image to focus only on the affected region.
3 Click Analyze with AI.
4 Review your AI-generated report and recommendations.

Supported Image Formats

JPG
JPEG
PNG

Tips for Best Accuracy

  • Use good lighting while taking the photo.
  • Keep the camera steady to avoid blur.
  • Capture only the affected skin region.
  • Avoid shadows and reflections.
  • Use high-resolution images whenever possible.
  • Ensure the lesion is clearly visible.

How AI Analysis Works

Upload

User uploads a skin image.

Preprocessing

Image is resized and normalized.

Deep Learning

MobileNetV2 extracts image features.

Prediction

AI predicts the most probable disease.

Report

A complete medical-style report is generated.
